Welcome To Our Badminton Club

This is a group made up of badminton enthusiast in the B.C lower-mainland region. We have several host that organize games through out the weekdays and weekends. We currently mainly hold games at Stage-18 Badminton and Drive Badminton. Doubles are the primary format of games until further notice. All skill levels are welcomed, though it is encourged to know badminton double's rule beforehand for everyone's enjoyment.
